About Totalsportek

Your premier destination for connecting with the world of live sports.

Our Mission

At Totalsportek, our mission is simple: to build a thriving community for sports lovers. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to watch their favorite teams and athletes compete, regardless of their location or budget. We are dedicated to creating a centralized hub where fans can easily find and access live sports streams from sources that are freely available on the internet.

What We Do

Totalsportek operates as a sophisticated search engine and directory for live sports streams. Our automated system constantly scours the web to discover publicly available broadcast links for a wide variety of sports, including Football, Basketball, UFC, and more. We then organize these links into a clean, user-friendly schedule, making it effortless for you to find the game you want to watch.

Our goal is to save you time and effort. Instead of searching through countless websites, you can come to Totalsportek and find a curated list of available streams for any given match, all in one place.

Disclaimer: Our Content Policy

It is crucial for our users to understand that Totalsportek does not host, control, or own any of the video content or streams featured on our website. Our platform, located at https://Totalsportekv2.world/, functions solely as a directory, providing hyperlinks to third-party streaming sources that are already accessible on the public internet.

We do not store any copyrighted material on our servers, nor do we participate in the uploading or distribution of such content. We are simply helping users find links that are already available online. Responsibility for the legality and content of these external streams lies entirely with the original hosts. We encourage all users to respect the copyright laws of their respective countries and to support official broadcasters whenever possible.
