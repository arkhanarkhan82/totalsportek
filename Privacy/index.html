Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 16, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Totalsportek ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("user," "you," or "your"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website, https://totalsportek.top/ (the "Site").

Our primary function is to act as a search engine for publicly available sports streams. We do not require user registration and do not collect personally identifiable information like your name, email address, or phone number.

Information We Collect

We automatically collect certain non-personal information when you access the Site to help us improve our services:

  • Log and Usage Data: This includes your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, pages you visit on our site, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages.
  • Cookies and Web Beacons: We use cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us understand your preferences for future visits. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies, but some parts of our Site may not function properly without them.

Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand our website traffic and user engagement. Google may use the collected data to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network. You can review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Advertising Partners: We may work with third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Site. These companies may use information (not including your name or email address) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

Third-Party Links

Our Site contains links to external websites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link (such as a stream source), you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with any information, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, the best way to reach us is by joining our Discord community. Our team is active there and ready to assist you.
